Prepaid card for business trips, vacations, employees and family with clear financial management

The CGN PAY card solution will offer both physical cards and virtual cards. The card is orientated to the businesses and individuals residents in eastern Europe. For safety of the clients funds the full cardholder's identification will be applied. All data and funds will be processed and stored by the Prepaid Financial Services Ltd (UK) and the Bancibo SE (CZ).

In cooperation with the card issuer the Prepaid Financial Services Ltd (UK) and the Bancibo SE (CZ) the new card will be presented in June 2020.

One of the owners and the CEO of the GCN PAY is Nikolaj Rozok. He has a broad financial background. Starting back in 2002 with the first financial institution under the FCA (UK) authorisation Nikolaj launched a number of the prepaid cards programs in Europe.

The owners of the GCN PAY have the goal to penetrate to the eastern Europe markets by the end of 2021. In cooperation with the card issuer the Prepaid Financial Services Ltd (UK) and the Bancibo SE (CZ) the new card will be presented in June 2020.
